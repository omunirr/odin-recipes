Shawarma Recipe

An easy recipe for the best shawarma dish

A delicious yet easy recipe for shawarma lovers

Ingredients

    1/2KG chicken breast
    120gm yogurt
    1 spoon of mixed masala or chicken masala
    1 tea spoon of chilli powder(if you're a spicy fan)
    A mid size onion and tomato
    A garlic
    1\2 tea spoon of salt

Steps

    Slice the onion and the tomato into small pieces
    Slice the breast mid size cubes
    mix the masala with yogurt and chilli powder and salt
    add the chicken to this mix
    heat the pan and add oil to it
    put the onion first and tomato after one to two minutes
    add the chicken
    mix and wait until the mix start to turn into oil and chicken be well-done
